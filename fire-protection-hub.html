Full Lifecycle

From Registration to Inspection

Every step of a fire safety project — managed, documented, and compliant — on a single platform.

STEP 01
🏢

Register by Company Size

Companies register with an annual fee scaled to their size (micro, small, medium, large). Upload Civil Defence certificates for verification.

STEP 02
📋

Post or Attend Tenders

Building owners post tenders with a listing fee. Certified companies receive instant notifications and submit competitive bids through the platform.

STEP 03
🤝

Award & Sign Contracts

Review bids, select the best company, and sign a digital contract — all within the platform with full legal audit trail and e-signature.

STEP 04
📝

Submit JCR Reports

After work is completed, the company submits a Job Completion Report (JCR). The building owner reviews and e-signs to confirm completion.

STEP 05
💳

Invoice Through Platform

Companies submit invoices tied to the contract. Owners approve and payments are tracked. Full financial history is stored in the platform.

STEP 06
🔔

Inspection & Renewal Alerts

6 months after each contract, both parties receive automatic inspection reminders. License expiry notifications sent 60, 30, and 7 days in advance.

What is a JCR?

A Job Completion Report (JCR) is the official record confirming that fire safety work has been carried out to specification. It includes the work scope, inspection checklist, Civil Defence permit number, and must be co-signed by both the company engineer and building owner before invoicing can proceed.
